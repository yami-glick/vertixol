What We Do

Our multidisciplinary expertise covers physical security, engineering risk assessments, comprehensive risk management, and management consulting.

Physical Security

On-site security guards and patrol services, access control systems (biometric, card readers, turnstiles), CCTV surveillance systems with real-time monitoring, perimeter security and intrusion detection systems, emergency response planning and execution, security procedures for events, high-risk sites, and VIP protection.

Engineering Risk Assessment

Structural integrity analysis, fire safety and evacuation planning, assessment of security system design and effectiveness, evaluation of access points, barriers, and emergency routes, identification of environmental and operational hazards, compliance audits with local and international building codes.

Risk Management

Enterprise risk management (ERM) frameworks, threat and vulnerability assessments (TVA), business continuity planning (BCP) and disaster recovery strategies, crisis management planning and training, supply chain risk analysis, regulatory compliance and risk reporting.

Management Consulting

Organizational structure analysis and improvement, process optimization and workflow redesign, change management support, security policy development and staff training, project management for security and infrastructure projects, development of security governance frameworks.

Integrated Solutions
